We are looking for volunteers to participate in our research on how the human mind can directly control and adapt within a videogame through a collaborative Brain Computer Interface (BCI). In this study, participants play a neurofeedback game where EEG sensors translate brain signals into in-game actions allowing control purely through thought.

Players will be assigned randomly to play individually or cooperatively, joining forces to complete shared tasks using synchronized brain activity.
